    Low Rank Method and Its Applications into Video/Image Processing

    时间：2016年7月4日（下周一）上午10:00-11:00

    地点：电子信息学院5-406会议室

    报告人：美国西弗吉尼亚大学计算机科学与电机工程系Prof. Xin Li

    报告摘要：

    Low rank matrix completion is a method that has received increasingly more attention in the past five years. It is related to the classical tool of singular value decomposition (SVD) but its connections with modeling important visual structures (e.g., textures in image and motion in video) are only recently discovered. In this talk, I will explore the insight behind low-rank based modeling of image/video signals and demonstrate two successful applications using low-rank method: image restoration and video segmentation/saliency detection.

    报告人概述：

    Xin Li received the B.S. degree with highest honors in electronic engineering and information science from University of Science and Technology of China, Hefei, in 1996, and the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from Princeton University, Princeton, NJ, in 2000. He was a Member of Technical Staff with Sharp Laboratories of America, Camas, WA from Aug. 2000 to Dec. 2002. Since Jan. 2003, he has been a faculty member in Lane Department of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering. His research interests include image/video coding and processing. Dr. Li received several paper awards for his contributions to the area of image and video processing. He is currently serving as a member of Image, Video and Multidimensional Signal Processing (IVMSP) Technical Committee.
